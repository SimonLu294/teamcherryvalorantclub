About Us

Team Cherry Valorant

The home of Cherry Esports

NFounded in 2025, Cherry Valorant Club is a competitive-minded gaming community based in Oakville. We bring players together across all ranks with a shared passion for Valorant. The club features a low-tier pro league roster competing in tier-4 and tier-3 tournaments. Mainly playing with Academy and Open Ladder teams where members of every rank can learn, grow, and have fun together.

Beyond competition, the club organizes structured training sessions such as aim routines, DEMO reviews, and IGL clinics to help players sharpen their skills. Members also enjoy VCT watch parties, in-house tournaments, and a supportive environment that bridges casual play with professional ambition. Our vision is to create a home where aspiring pros and everyday players alike can connect, improve, and celebrate the game they love together.
